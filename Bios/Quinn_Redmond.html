A Wheaton College Class of 2018 member, Quinn is an Art History major. Her interest in Art History stemmed from a love of the Classics and Classical Literature. At Wheaton she works in the Permanent Collection as a collection assistent, giving her access to both the school's art and the skills of collection management. During the summer of 2016, she interned at the Attleboro Arts Museum, a non-profit near Wheaton's campus. This has allowed her to further immerse herself in the art world outside of Wheaton. This excitement for both travel and learning will certainly stay with her. One day Quinn aspires to see the Sagrada Familia in it's hoped completion.
