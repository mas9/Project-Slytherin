Fai Chonnipa Pokachaiyapat will graduate from Wheaton College in May of 2017 with a Bachelor of Arts in Art History and Italian Studies Minor. Originally from Bangkok, Thailand, she chose to continue her studies at Wheaton College to expand her cultural knowledge, as well as, language proficiency. She currently works at Wheaton's Collection Study Room as a collection assistantance. Her interests in Museum Studies comes from her interest in education. Previously, she has worked with Teach For Thailand's organization, as well as, volunteer teaching in the northern part of Thailand. In her spare time she likes to draw, painting, and take photography. Museum Studies allows education and art to be combined. She hopes to use both her passion in Art, and education to pursue a career in the museums studies after graduating from Wheaton College.
